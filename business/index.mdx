## How verification works for businesses

Business accounts go through KYB (Know Your Business) on top of standard KYC for each beneficial owner.

**Required at signup:**

- Legal name and registration number of the entity.
- Country of incorporation.
- Beneficial-owner details for any individual with &gt;25% ownership.
- A short description of the business.

**Median verification time** is the same business day. Complex multi-entity structures or unusual jurisdictions may take 2&ndash;3 days.

See [Identity verification](/accounts/identity) for what each individual needs to provide.
